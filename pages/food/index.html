When visiting Balikpapan, don't forget that there is Gulung Jenebora which provides a variety of delicious, healthy, and various Balikpapan souvenirs. Gulung Jenebora has several variants, including Bolu Roll variant with delicious dragon fruit jam, then there are various variants of delicious brownies, and delicious pandan bingka.

Balikpapan is a city that is rich in marine products, one of which is crab. Balikpapan souvenirs that are the main attraction are Crab Shredded or commonly called bonting. Bonting is a typical food of Balikpapan which is very tasty and delicious, no wonder this food is always a target for tourists who are visiting this city.

Dandito Crab Restaurant is one of the most famous places to eat crabs and other seafood in Balikpapan City. Moreover, the location is in a quite strategic street. Everyone who comes to this city, definitely does not escape from stopping by this restaurant. Various crabs are processed with various sauces. Starting from Black Pepper Sauce, Sweet Sour Sauce, Oyster Sauce, to Dandito Special Sauce. Or the crab meat of your choice can also be fried or even boiled.

Amplang is a kind of cracker that can be eaten as a complement to heavy meals or as a snack originating from East Borneo, especially Balikpapan. These amplang crackers are made from a mixture of tapioca flour, mackerel or flat fish plus spices. Different from other regions, amplang from Balikpapan tastes more fishy.
