Tourist Attraction

Titik Nol is located at Sepaku, North Penajam Paser, East Borneo. Titik Nol IKN is a monument located in the Capital of Indonesia. This monument was built in February 2022 which is the beginning of the construction of the Capital of Indonesia and is intended to be part of the History of Indonesia's rise towards "Indonesia Gold 2045". This place is often used as a photo location for tourists because of its beautiful natural scenery.

Melawai Beach has become an icon or hangout place for young people living in Balikpapan and tourism on the Indonesian island of Borneo. This place has become famous because of its easy-to-reach location and the typical view of the city of Balikpapan which is displayed every day as if this beach is a reflection of the city of Balikpapan itself. The location of Melawai beach which is in a small bay, and the view is not limited to anything, so the sunset can be seen perfectly and beautiful from this place.

Manggar Segara Sari Beach Balikpapan is a beach tourism in Balikpapan which is located 20 km from the city center. With the estimation of the beauty of the expanse of the sea and its white sand, visitors will feel at home to linger in this place. Therefore, when vacation time arrives, it is certain that thousands of people flock to vacation there. In addition, there are many other attractions owned by Segara Sari Beach, so it is highly recommended for you to take the time to go to this place. If you are lucky, the sunset that looks hidden behind the sea surface can also be enjoyed directly.

Lamaru Beach is one of the alternative tourism in Balikpapan. Presents a beautiful view of the Makassar Strait. This beach is a favorite destination for the people of Balikpapan and its surroundings who want to relax in the wild. This tourist attraction is very famous for the rows of cypress trees along the beach. Tourists will also be spoiled with various facilities that are more complete than beach attractions in general. This beach which provides a variety of tourist facilities is visited by many because its location is also easily accessible. The attractiveness and complete beach facilities make this tourist area highly recommended for vacations.
